A Story of Two Worlds

My design philosophy is shaped by a unique blend of cultures, perspectives, and experiences from my life in both India and Germany.

Bridging Perspectives

Living and studying in both Maharashtra, India, and Siegen, Germany has given me a unique lens. This cross-cultural experience is the foundation of my commitment to creating inclusive, adaptable, and deeply human user experiences.

My Story

Hey there, I’m Priyanshu — a curious mind on a mission to transform tech from frustrating to freeing, from cold interfaces to warm, human moments.

It Started With a Few Questions...

Why does using some apps feel like a struggle?

Why do we accept “confusing” as normal?

What if tech could really understand us?

This Led Me Down a New Path...

Building the Foundation

I dove into Computer Applications, but code alone couldn’t satisfy my craving to understand people.

The Leap into the Real World

I started freelancing and designing, learning to listen as much as I build.

A New Philosophy

Every project became a lesson in empathy and creating with heart — because design is more than pixels, it’s people.

Real-World Stories That Shaped Me

UX Intern in MedTech @ Carl Zeiss AG

Feb 2024 – Aug 2024

A game changer. I designed with precision and empathy for healthcare products, ran usability tests with doctors, and conducted research that shaped the future of MedTech. This was where I saw design’s power to impact real lives.

Support Engineer @ Screen Magic Media

Jun 2023 – Oct 2023

Behind the scenes magic. I mastered CRM systems and automation tools like Salesforce and ZOHO, smoothing workflows so users got seamless experiences without even noticing the gears turning.

UX Designer @ Ozibook

Dec 2022 – Feb 2023

My first playground. I led a tiny but mighty design team, ran user research, and shipped a product that users actually loved. That’s when I realized — design isn’t decoration, it’s connection.

Freelance UX Designer

2021 – 2022

Jack of many trades: researcher, designer, project wrangler, and sometimes unofficial user therapist. Learned how to keep creativity alive under deadlines, and how to let real problems lead the way.

My Guiding Principles

I listen more than I speak

Because most of what matters is never said aloud. True understanding begins in silence.

I don't fix everything

I first ask if it should exist at all. Intention matters more than action.

Clarity is a form of love

If I can help someone feel less confused and more seen, I’ve done something valuable.

Living between languages taught me the design of empathy

When words fail, you read eyes, tone, posture. That’s where my empathy sharpened.

Simplicity is not lack. It's wisdom

I've let go of what doesn't serve. In that space, I've found what matters.

I design better questions

There is no perfect answer—only better framing. The right question can change everything.

Redesigning my life taught me more than any classroom

Moving away from home wasn’t just a relocation—it was a redefinition. The most meaningful design is the one you live.
